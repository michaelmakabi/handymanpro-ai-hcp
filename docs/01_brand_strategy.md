# Brand Strategy — HandyMan Pro LLC

## 1. What the company actually does

A South Florida (Weston / Pembroke Pines / Davie) full-service remodeling firm. Not a "handyman" in the Task-Rabbit sense — a remodeling general contractor with a unified crew. Run by **Moses** (project manager / principal), with handpicked crew. Existing site: handymanprofl.com. Instagram: @handymanprofl.

**Categories served:**
- Kitchen remodels (from $4,999 partial / full kitchens custom-quoted)
- Bathroom remodels — tub/shower from $4,999, guest bath package from $7,999
- Flooring (tile, vinyl plank, hardwood, laminate)
- Drywall — hang, finish, patch, texture match
- Interior & exterior painting
- Popcorn ceiling removal (smooth / knockdown / skim coat)
- Water damage restoration (24/7 response)
- Walk-in closet systems
- Full-home renovations
- Residential + commercial

## 2. Revenue model

Project-based labor + materials. Typical ticket: **$4,999 – $80,000+**. Cash, financing, and (target) deposit via Stripe for hero offers.

## 3. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Primary ICP — "Settled Homeowner Maria"**
- Female, 38–62, lives in Weston / Pembroke Pines / Davie or surrounding
- Household income $120K+
- Owns the home, lived in it 3+ years
- Trigger event: dated kitchen/bath, child moved out (refresh), water damage, hurricane prep, planned sale within 18 months
- Hates: getting ghosted by contractors, surprise change orders, dust everywhere, multiple subs blaming each other
- Lives on: Instagram (saves remodel inspo), Nextdoor (asks for contractor recs), Google Maps (reads reviews obsessively)

**Secondary ICP — "Pragmatic Property Manager Daniel"**
- Manages 5–40 South Florida residential units
- Needs same-week turnover work between tenants
- Trigger event: tenant move-out, insurance claim, code violation
- Hates: contractors who can't invoice properly, no W-9, no insurance certificate, slow callback

## 4. Unique mechanism

**Same-day, single-crew, fixed-price remodeling — captured 24/7 by an AI receptionist that never sleeps.**

Three moats fused:
1. **One project manager (Moses)** — single point of contact through the entire job
2. **Fixed price up front** — no scope-creep change orders, no "surprise" line items
3. **AI front desk** — Retell + GHL respond in under 60 seconds, 24/7, in English or Spanish (South Florida bilingual is mandatory)

## 5. Irresistible offer framework (Hormozi grand-slam)

### Hero offer #1 — $4,999 tub or shower remodel
- **Dream outcome:** New tub or shower in 5 business days, no surprise costs.
- **Perceived likelihood:** "Guaranteed fixed price — written quote within 24 hours of measure."
- **Time delay:** Demo in 48 hours of contract signed. Finished in 5 business days.
- **Effort & sacrifice:** Single AI call, single measure visit. No back-and-forth scheduling.
- **Stack-on bonuses:**
  - Free in-home design consult ($297 value)
  - 12-month workmanship warranty
  - Daily site clean-up
  - Free post-job touch-up visit at day 30

### Hero offer #2 — $7,999 guest bathroom turn-key
- Same framework, full-bathroom scope (vanity + tile + paint + fixtures + lighting).

### Hero offer #3 — Free 60-second AI estimate
- "Call (754) 247-6766 and our AI gives you a ballpark in 60 seconds. No human. No pressure. No callback unless you ask."
- This is the **wedge** — converts top-of-funnel browsers into qualified leads without burning Moses's time.

## 6. Emotional buying drivers (use in copy)

| Driver | What they actually fear | Copy that defuses it |
|---|---|---|
| Permits | "I'm going to get fined by the city or HOA" | "All permits pulled by us. Included." |
| Change orders | "The price is going to triple" | "Fixed price. In writing. Before we start." |
| Disappearing contractor | "What if they take my deposit and ghost me?" | "Family-run since [year]. Same crew, same number — always answered." |
| Dust & damage | "My whole house will be covered in plaster" | "Plastic-walled work zone. Daily clean-up. White-glove finish." |
| Permits / inspections delays | "It'll drag on for months" | "5-day bath remodel. Written timeline guarantee." |
| Language barrier | "My crew won't understand what I want" | "Bilingual project management. English and Spanish, every conversation." |

## 7. Competitor scan — South Florida remodeling

| Competitor | Position | Weakness we exploit |
|---|---|---|
| Re-Bath, Bath Fitter (franchises) | National brand, tub liners only | Limited scope, high price, no full-bath |
| Home Depot Home Services | One-stop convenience | Subcontracted crews, slow scheduling, no project manager |
| Solo handymen on Thumbtack/Angi | Lowest price | No insurance, no warranty, no after-sales support |
| Custom GC firms | Premium full remodels | $50K minimums, slow estimates, no $4,999 offer |

**Our gap to occupy:** the "$5K to $25K" sweet spot — bigger than a handyman, smaller than a custom GC, faster and more accountable than either.
