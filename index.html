Frequently asked

Everything homeowners ask before they say yes.

Do you pull permits? +

Yes — every job that requires one. The permit cost is included in the written quote. We handle the application, inspections, and final sign-off so you don't have to.

How long does a typical bathroom remodel take? +

The $4,999 tub or shower remodel is finished in 5 business days. The $7,999 guest bathroom package is finished in 10 business days. Both timelines are in writing on your contract.

What is your workmanship warranty? +

12 months on labor. We come back and fix anything we built that fails — no charge, no debate. Manufacturer warranties on installed materials run separately and we handle those claims for you.

Are there going to be surprise change orders? +

No. Your written quote is fixed. If we discover something mid-job that wasn't visible at the measure visit, we absorb it. The only exception is if you change the scope — and even then, you sign a fresh fixed-price addendum before we proceed.

Do you offer financing? +

Yes — third-party financing through approved partners, with terms starting as low as $89/month for the $4,999 offer. Moses walks through your options at the estimate visit. We never push it.

How clean is your work zone? +

Plastic-walled, taped at the edges. Drop cloths over flooring. Vacuum and wipe-down at the end of every shift. We leave your house cleaner than the crew found it.

Which cities do you serve? +

Weston, Pembroke Pines, Davie, and the surrounding Broward County area (Cooper City, Hollywood, Plantation, Sunrise, Miramar). If you're outside those zip codes, call us — we may still cover you depending on the project size.

What about emergencies — water, storm, structural? +

Call (754) 247-6766 24/7. Maya routes water and structural calls directly to Moses's cell. We typically have demo started within 6 hours of your call.

¿Hablan español? +

Sí. Maya, nuestra recepcionista virtual, contesta en español automáticamente. Moses y la mayoría del equipo hablan español. Todas las comunicaciones, contratos y presupuestos están disponibles en ambos idiomas.

Do you work with property managers and commercial clients? +

Yes — turnover work, tenant-move-in remodels, commercial build-outs. Fixed-price packets per unit, W-9 and insurance certificate on file, invoiced cleanly. Ask for our PM rate sheet.
