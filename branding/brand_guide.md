# Brand Guide — HandyMan Pro LLC × Loren AI

## Brand essence

> **One crew. One price. One number that always picks up.**

HandyMan Pro is the antidote to the "fly-by-night handyman" reflex every South Florida homeowner has — solved with a single project manager (Moses), fixed-price quotes, and an AI receptionist that never lets a lead die in voicemail.

## Voice

- **Confident, not loud.** We promise specific dollar amounts and specific timelines because we hit them.
- **Operator, not influencer.** No "amazing," no exclamation marks, no emoji.
- **Local.** Weston, Pembroke Pines, Davie — names matter. South Florida heat, hurricane season, HOA rules — context matters.

## Color palette

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `--ink` | `#0B0E14` | Primary background (dark luxury) |
| `--ink-soft` | `#11151F` | Card surfaces |
| `--bone` | `#F5F1E8` | Body text on dark / soft hero |
| `--brand` | `#E8B257` | Primary accent (warm brass — premium, not Home Depot orange) |
| `--brand-deep` | `#9C6B1F` | Hover / pressed |
| `--steel` | `#6B7280` | Secondary text, dividers |
| `--success` | `#3FB68C` | Trust badges, "in budget" indicators |
| `--alert` | `#E2574C` | Urgency, limited-time only |

Rationale: dark navy ink + brass accent = premium remodeling (Restoration Hardware, not Lowe's). Avoid orange/red — that's the budget tier.

## Typography

- **Headings:** `Inter Tight`, weight 700, tracking -0.02em
- **Body:** `Inter`, weight 400/500
- **Numerals (prices):** `Inter`, tabular nums, weight 600
- **Mono (specs, addresses):** `JetBrains Mono`, weight 500

All Google Fonts — load `display=swap`.

## Logo lockup

Wordmark only for now. "HandyMan Pro" in Inter Tight 700, tight tracking, brass accent on "Pro." Tagline beneath in Inter 500 caps: "REMODELING — SOUTH FLORIDA."

## Photography direction

- Real jobs only. No stock.
- Before/after pairs, shot from the same angle.
- Daylight, no HDR, no oversaturation.
- Include Moses or a crew member in 1 of every 4 images — humanizes.

## Required footer

Every page must end with:

```
Powered by LOREN AI
```

In `--steel` color, small caps, centered. Per CLAUDE.md non-negotiable.

## Spacing system

8px base. Card padding 24px (mobile) / 32px (desktop). Section vertical rhythm 96px (mobile) / 144px (desktop).

## Radius

12px on inputs, 16px on cards, 999px on pills. Never sharp corners except dividers.
